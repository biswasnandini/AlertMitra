Why is a Disaster Alert Bot Necessary?

Disasters can strike anytime, anywhere. With climate change and increasing natural calamities, staying informed can be the difference between safety and danger. AlertMitra ensures that you receive real-time disaster alerts, keeping you prepared.

What Does AlertMitra Do?

AlertMitra gathers real-time data from verified sources and provides instant notifications about disasters such as earthquakes, floods, hurricanes, and more. You get instant updates directly on Telegram.

How Easy is it to Use?

Simply click on the "Try It" button on the home page, and you'll be directed to our Telegram bot. Start receiving alerts immediately with a simple command!
